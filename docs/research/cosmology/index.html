Cosmology
I am currently most interested in the relationship between the dark energy field in ΛCDM and Einstein's cosmological constant, particularly the possibility that renormalization of λ could have measurable predictions when compared to data from strong gravitational lensing. My work with SDSS includes measurements of large-scale structure, the evolution of structure, and improved cosmological constraints.
