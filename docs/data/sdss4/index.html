SDSS-IV
I am currently working on the final data release for SDSS-IV, including a number of value-added catalogs related to the Spectroscopic Identification of Lensing Objects (SILO) and other data analysis projects (with research publications currently in preparation), updating the Science Archive Webapp for the data release, and managing the SDSS-IV science archive server, which includes a variety of systems managed by the Utah Center for High Performance Computing (CHPC).
Extended Baryon Oscillation Spectroscopic Survey (eBOSS)

My interest in eBOSS includes the study of millions of galaxies which have optical spectra obtained with a single spectroscopic fiber, which can be searched, viewed or downloaded using the SDSS-IV Science Archive Optical Webapp.

More info...
Mapping Nearby Galaxies at APO (MaNGA)

My interest in MaNGA includes the study of 10,000 nearby galaxies, which have multiple optical spectra obtained with a bundle of spectroscopic fibers distributed across the visible plane of the galaxy, which can be searched, viewed, or downloaded using the SDSS-IV Marvin Webapp on the Science Archive Server.

More info...
APO Galactic Evolution Experiment 2 (APOGEE-2)

My interest in APOGEE-2 includes the study of hundreds of thousands of stars, which have infrared spectra obtained in multiple visits, which can be searched, viewed, or downloaded using the Science Archive Infrared Webapp.

The Spectroscopic Identification of Lensing Objects (SILO) Survey
